# Sponsors

universal-spawn is open source and will remain so. Sponsorship funds
maintainer time, independent audits of the specification, conformance
test infrastructure, and community events.

Sponsor via
[GitHub Sponsors on @JanSol0s](https://github.com/sponsors/JanSol0s),
or reach out to `sponsors@agentmindcloud.org` for tier arrangements.

## Tiers

### Platform Partner

For platforms that consume universal-spawn manifests. Platform Partners
receive:

- Early review of spec proposals that affect their extension.
- A seat in the quarterly conformance review.
- Logo placement in this file and on release announcements.
- Co-branded conformance badge.

Platform Partners **do not** receive special treatment in spec votes
or the right to veto community proposals. Governance rules in
[`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md) always take precedence.

### Ecosystem Partner

For registries, validator vendors, tooling companies, and training
platforms building on top of universal-spawn. Ecosystem Partners
receive:

- Public listing on the README partners section.
- Access to pre-release schema drafts for compatibility testing.
- Credit in release notes.

### Individual Supporter

For individuals supporting the project. Individual Supporters receive:

- A name on the "Thank you" page (this file) unless they opt out.
- Early access to the Discord community roles.

## What sponsorship does not buy

- **Not** a field in the specification named after you.
- **Not** a fast-track for a platform folder or a schema change.
- **Not** a vote in governance beyond what any contributor has.
- **Not** removal of critical issues or discussions.

The integrity of the standard is worth more than any single sponsor
check. If these terms are unacceptable, sponsorship is probably not
the right fit.
