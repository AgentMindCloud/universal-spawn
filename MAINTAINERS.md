who currently holds the role. It is referenced by
[`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md) and

## What a maintainer does

- Reviews PRs on their focus area. Target: two working days to first
  response.
- Triages issues: labels, requests missing info, closes out-of-scope.
- Approves changes in line with the decision rules in
  [`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md).
- Shepherds RFCs from proposal → merge.
- Participates in the monthly maintainer sync (30 minutes, video).

Expected time commitment: 2–4 hours per week. The role is voluntary
unless the maintainer is sponsored (see [`SPONSORS.md`](SPONSORS.md)).

## How to become a maintainer

1. Land at least **five** substantive PRs against this repository.
   "Substantive" means a spec edit, a platform folder, an example
   set, a validator contribution, or a significant docs improvement.
2. Demonstrate review quality. Comment on open PRs with constructive,
   technically specific feedback.
3. After the above, any current maintainer can nominate you. The
   Editor plus one other maintainer approves the nomination.
4. On acceptance, your handle is added to this file and to
   `.github/CODEOWNERS` in the PR that nominates you.

## Stepping down

A maintainer may step down at any time with a PR removing their row
from this file. Inactive maintainers (no reviews or issue activity for
six months) are moved to the "Emeritus" section below by consent;
emeritus maintainers keep credit and may return anytime.
