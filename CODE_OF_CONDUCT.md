# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We — contributors and maintainers of universal-spawn — pledge to make
participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone. We do this not as a slogan but as a prerequisite for good
engineering: disagreement is productive only when people feel safe
enough to disagree.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior we expect:

- Direct, technical, courteous communication.
- Disagreement aimed at arguments, not at people.
- Assuming other contributors are acting in good faith until proven
  otherwise.
- Clear writing, well-structured issues, reproducible PRs.
- Acknowledging when someone else is right.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, insults, or demeaning language.
- Harassment — public or private — including targeted unwelcome
  contact, doxxing, and sexualized attention.
- Discriminatory jokes or language about any protected class.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or reviews.
- Publishing private correspondence or unpublished vulnerabilities.

## Scope

This code applies to every project space: issues, PRs, discussions,
code review, and any public channel where someone represents
universal-spawn.

## Enforcement

Reports go to `conduct@agentmindcloud.org`. All reports are handled
confidentially. The Editor (or a delegate with no conflict of
interest) reviews each report and may take any of the following
actions:

1. A private clarification or warning.
2. A public correction with expected behavior spelled out.
3. A temporary ban from project spaces (days to weeks).
4. A permanent ban from project spaces.

Decisions can be appealed by emailing the same address; an appeal is
reviewed by a maintainer other than the original deciding party.
